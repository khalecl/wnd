  • Sound power levels at different wind speeds (IEC 61400-11 data)

  • Hub height and rotor diameter specifications

  • Site boundary coordinates
  • Access road positions and widths
  • Construction compound locations

Please provide this information within 10 business days to avoid delays in permit application submission.

Best regards,
Environmental Assessment Team
